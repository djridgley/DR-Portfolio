My Story

Starting in May 2017 I began a journey where for a little over a year I learned Web Development. It started off as a thing on the side, 10-15hrs per week on my days off. I wanted to see if it was something I enjoyed, and as it turns out I really did. In July 2017 I decided to commit to it full time and left my job. I had enough savings to get by for awhile if I lived cheap, and I wanted to give this my all. I spent 30-40hrs per week every week learning new things and improving existing skills. In June 2018 I obtained my first dev job as a UI Developer at Aunalytics.

I worked as a UI developer for around 6 months before transitioning into a new role. I currently work as a Front End QA Engineer. My main role is creating and managing testing using CypressIO. CypressIO is a powerful testing library used for creating end-to-end and integration testing. I also work with Jenkins managing releases assuring all front end features are thorougly QA'd and pass all testing before making it into production. I'm happy where I am and not currently seeking new job opportunities.

I'm proficient writing HTML and JavaScript. I have worked with other frameworks such as React, Vue and Backbone in the past, but it has been nearly a year so I would need a refresher on them before I would consider myself competent at developing with them.

Goals

I spent a little over a year learning what I needed to secure a position as a Jr. Developer. In June of 2018 I achieved that obtaining a position as a UI Developer at Aunalytics.

After 6 months I transitioned into the position of Front End QA Engineer and became solely responsible for creating end to end testing for the webapp front end. In August 2019 we finally got a second QA teammate and we now work together creating/updating testing and making sure all Front End features get thoroughly QA'd before making it to production.
