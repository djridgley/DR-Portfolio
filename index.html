My Story

Starting in May 2017 I began a journey where for a little over a year I learned Web Development. It started off as a thing on the side, 10-15hrs per week on my days off. I wanted to see if it was something I enjoyed, and as it turns out I really did. In July 2017 I decided to commit to it full time and left my job. I had enough savings to get by for awhile if I lived cheap, and I wanted to give this my all. I spent 30-40hrs per week every week learning new things and improving existing skills. In June 2018 I obtained my first dev job as a UI Developer at Aunalytics.

I worked as a UI developer for around 6 months before transitioning into a new role. Due to my interest in testing and QA I often worked on testing and automation even as a UI Developer as we had no QA team. Eventually my role was shifted and the new QA team was formed with me as the first member.

My main role was creating and managing testing using Webdriver IO initially. After a bit I shifted the whole suite over to CypressIO as it had better performance. CypressIO is a powerful testing library used for creating end-to-end and integration testing. I was also pivotal in developing and implementing a QA procedure for our development pipeline as they had no QA process at the time. In addition to those tasks I also worked heavily with Jenkins managing releases assuring all automated testing was passing before builds were deployed to the different environments. Lastly, there was a handful of training I did as new members joined the QA team. Teaching them our process and/or Cypress IO where applicable.

So that brings us to the present. I have moved on from my previous employer and I'm looking for a new home. I would prefer to work remote or within 30 miles of South Bend. I am willing to relocate in the future, but it would be mid 2022 at the earliest before I can relocate. Please reach out to me via email or LinkedIn to get in contact, thanks!

Goals

I made the decision in early 2017 that I wanted to change my career and my future. I knew I was capable of more and a good friend got me started with C# and I discovered I really enjoy programming.

I spent a little over a year learning what I needed to secure a position as a Jr. Developer. In June of 2018 I achieved that obtaining a position as a UI Developer at Aunalytics.

In August 2019 we finally got a second QA teammate and we worked together creating/updating testing and making sure all Front End features got thoroughly tested before making it to production.
